To play, you need a valid username. This is be obtained from http://localhost:8080/register. Use this username for the entire play session.
To mark the space, just right click your mouse on the box you want to mark. "X" mark goes first.
To win the game, you have to make three of your marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.
